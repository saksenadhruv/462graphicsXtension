Over assignment 3 key controls we have also new keys in RENDER mode (should've pressed R)

Key Effect
n/N Decrease k or radius
m/M Increase k or radius
l/L num_photons/10
;/: num_photons*10
j/J num_photons*2
k/K toggle k-nn/radial search
p/P toggle bi-directional
